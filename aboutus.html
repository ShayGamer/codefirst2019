About Us

Alternative Wear is a British fashion retailer, that caters to people with a interesting sense of Fashion. Founded by Sharna and Samara, known as Shay and Sam

Founders

Alternative Wear was Founded by Sharna and Samara, known as Shay and Sam. Both were students when they first started learning about HTML and code, and decided they wanted to make a website for people with different fashion styles. The kind of styles that stick out, and make you remember the person. Both Sam and Shay have achieved this in their daily lifes, and wanted to create a platfrom where others could do this also.
